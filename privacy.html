Privacy Policy

KlaiChan Property collects and processes the following data:

  • TikTok account identifiers
  • TikTok authorization tokens
  • Media files (videos or images) provided for posting
  • Text content related to property listings

This data is used solely to publish content to TikTok on behalf of the user.

Data is processed through automation infrastructure such as n8n and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Users may request data deletion by contacting: klaichanproperty@gmail.com
